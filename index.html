I'm an amateur polymath trying to make sense out of nonsense. I was born in Charleston, South Carolina; studied liberal arts at St. John's College in Annapolis, Maryland; and studied math at the College of Charleston, and the University of Oregon, in Eugene, Oregon. I am deeply interested in mathematics, particularly geometry, economics, physics, biology, and really any way of grappling with the complexity of the world we live in. I spend my free time playing video games, listening to podcasts, programming, reading, and hanging out with my friends.
